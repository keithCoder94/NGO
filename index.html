GIRL CHILD EMPOWERMENT

Empowering a girl child to reach her full potential
by staying in school.

OUR STORY

Limpho Phalelo Foundation was founded in 2021 by five kind-hearted women who saw the need of keeping a girl child in school. These women believe that no girl child should stay out of school because of lack of sanitary towels, toiletries and other school essentials. They have made it their vision to try and better the lives young girls.


OUR MISSION

Girls face incredible opportunities and challenges as they grow. At Limpho-Phalelo Foundation we are dedicated to empowering girls by ensuring that they stays in school and reach their full potential through learning, we also aim to provide a comprehensive service to ensure a dignified lifestyle.

OUR VISION

To build a Foundation where all girls do not feel excluded but rather to feel loved, respected and encouraged to develop to their fullest potential and to see to it that a girl child is developed by staying in school.

DONATE FOR A GOOD CAUSE

Women and girls are often left out entirely and they suffer the most, we believe that girls are the key to a long term change that will break the cycle of poverty. That is why we have devoted our time to help a girl child stay in school.

Our Programmes

At Limpho-Phalelo Foundation we have dedicated our time to
running the following programmes:

Girl Child Mentoring

Each one of our five members has chosen two girls in their respective neighborhood to mentor.
This includes helping them with homework, assignments and school projects and also advising them about life. They believe that with their help the girls will soar to greater heights.

Girls Support Group

This is where the members and the girls will come together and talk about their issues. The support group entails listening and giving advise where it is needed. This helps both the members and the girls to offload their problems, it also give them a positive perspective in life.

Fund Raising

The members come together and identify a spot where they can occupy in order to sell food to make enough money that is meant for buying the school essentials that the girls will need. The members also ask for any form of donations from the community members and other funding agencies.
